Hi, there.

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Johns Hopkins Whiting School of Engineering working with Dr. Paul Ferraro. From 2019-2021, I was a Harvard Data Science Initiative Postdoctoral Fellow at the Harvard TH Chan School of Public Health. I earned a PhD in Biology and MS in Statistics from Auburn University.

My research interests broadly cover the area of statistical methods for ecological research, particularly developing new techniques to quantify climate change effects on populations and identifying causation in ecological data. I also develop robust statistical methods for spatiotemporal and high-dimensional data.
