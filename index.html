Welcome

I am a Biology PhD candidate at Auburn University with an MS in Statistics.

​My research interests are broadly in the area of statistical ecology, particularly developing new techniques to quantify climate change effects on populations. I also work on improving spatio-temporal statistical forecasting methods and identifying causation in ecological data.
